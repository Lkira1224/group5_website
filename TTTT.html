100 THIEVES

100 Thieves (abbreviated as "100T") is an American esports organization based in Los Angeles, California. It was founded by Call of Duty player and personality Matthew "Nadeshot" Haag and owned by the NBA team, Cleveland Cavaliers. 100 Thieves has teams competing in League of Legends, Call of Duty, Fortnite Battle Royale and Apex Legends..

T1

T1 (previously known as SK Telecom T1 or SKT T1) is a South Korean esports organization operated by the T1 Entertainment & Sports, a joint venture between SK Telecom and Comcast Spectacor. The team that would become SKT T1 was originally founded in 2002 by StarCraft player Lim "BoxeR"
